This is the management tool for BibTex Article references. You can save article, book and inproceedings type references and then view them. There are 14 entry types in the BibTex format and we recommend getting acquainted with them first if you are yet unfamiliar with BibTex.

Click on Submit to enter a reference, and then click on View to view all the saved references. Authors are separated in the BibTex format with the word "and".

(The website is a group project as part of the University of Helsinki course Ohjelmistotuotanto, code TKT20006. The group in charge of this site is JEEOVI.)
